How budgets work

Budgets are analytical comparisons and will not alter accounting transactions.

  • 1Overall budgets
    Include Bills, Expenses and Mileage.
  • 2Category budgets
    Include regular Expenses and Mileage.
  • 3All incurred spending
    Both paid and unpaid transactions will count.
  • 4Consistent values
    Actual spending will use transaction dates and gross amounts.
