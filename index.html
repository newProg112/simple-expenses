FAQs

Common questions about the Simple Books platform.

Who is Simple Books for?

Simple Books is designed for freelancers, sole traders and small businesses that want one place to manage work, finances and planning.

Can I export my data?

Yes. You can download backups and export your records whenever you want, so you stay in control of your information.

What financial reports are included?

Simple Books includes a Trial Balance, General Ledger, Profit & Loss and Balance Sheet, supported by connected business records.

Can I export my data for a financial handover?

Yes. Pro includes the Accountant Pack alongside structured exports and advanced reporting.

What is included in the free plan?

Starter includes core business features with limited AI usage, invoice scanning and live projects.

How do I get started?

Create an account, add your business details and start using the dashboard straight away.
