FAQs

Quick answers to common questions.

Do you work remotely?

Yes — most clients share documents digitally and we keep everything organised month by month.

Which software do you use?

Xero, QuickBooks, FreeAgent, and spreadsheet-based workflows — depending on what’s right for you.

Can you help if I’m behind?

Absolutely. Catch-up work is common; we’ll agree a plan and timeline first.

Do you file accounts or corporation tax?

I focus on clean bookkeeping figures and reports. If you need accounts/tax filing, I can work with your accountant or recommend one.
